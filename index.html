User Interface Software and Technology

Andrew J. Ko

The HCI community has a long history of innovation in user interface technologies, but there are few surveys or review of all of this research. This book attempts to summarize all of this knowledge, providing a living online textbook for UX designers, researchers, and design-minded engineers to learn from and leverage in their work.

I hope you enjoy! If not, please send me feedback.
